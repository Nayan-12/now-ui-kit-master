About Me.

I’m Nayan, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering, with a strong passion for developing efficient, user-centric software solutions. Throughout my academic journey, I’ve gained extensive knowledge and hands-on experience in various aspects of software development, especially in the realm of front-end development.

I have had the opportunity to intern at reputable companies such as Asterisk Technocrat and Fledge Consulting Pvt. Ltd., where I further sharpened my technical skills. During these internships, I focused on building responsive, intuitive user interfaces, working primarily with HTML5 and other modern web technologies. These experiences helped me not only enhance my expertise but also understand the nuances of creating products that provide seamless and satisfying user experiences.

One of the projects I am particularly proud of is the face recognition-based attendance system that I developed. This system incorporates advanced deep learning techniques to automatically recognize and mark attendance based on facial recognition. The system achieved an accuracy of over 96%, highlighting the robustness of the model and the effectiveness of the solution in a real-world setting. This project allowed me to delve deep into the fields of computer vision and machine learning, giving me a hands-on understanding of how to apply these technologies to solve complex problems.

I am constantly motivated by the challenges of solving intricate technical problems and providing innovative solutions through technology. Moving forward, I am eager to continue expanding my expertise in areas such as artificial intelligence (AI), cybersecurity, and web development. My focus remains on crafting solutions that are not only technically sound but also elevate the user experience to new heights.
